A little about who we are...

Welcome to Tsunami, the premier provider of canoeing fan merchandise.

We are a team of passionate canoeing enthusiasts who have come together to create a company that celebrates the thrill and excitement of canoeing. Our goal is to provide canoeing enthusiasts with the best quality merchandise that reflects their love for the sport.

We understand that canoeing is more than just a hobby, it's a way of life. That's why we take great care in selecting only the best materials and designs for our products. From canoe paddles to t-shirts, we have something for every canoeing fan.

At Tsunami, we are committed to providing exceptional customer service. Our team is always available to answer any questions you may have and to ensure your satisfaction.

We invite you to explore our range of canoeing merchandise and to join the Tsunami community. Let's celebrate the thrill and excitement of canoeing together!

FAQ

What materials are your canoes made of?

Our canoes are made of high-quality, durable materials such as fiberglass and carbon fiber. These materials provide the perfect balance of strength and weight, making our canoes suitable for a wide range of canoeing activities.

Do you offer custom designs for your canoes?

Yes, we offer custom design options for our canoes. You can choose from a wide range of colors and designs to create a canoe that is unique to you. Contact our customer service team for more information on customizing your canoe.

What is the warranty on your canoes?

We stand behind the quality of our canoes and offer a limited warranty on all of our products. Our warranty covers any defects in materials or workmanship for a period of 1 year from the date of purchase.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on our website or through the shipping carrier's website. If you have any questions or concerns about your order, please contact our customer service team.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. All items must be unused and in their original packaging. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

What is the lead time for delivery of my canoe?

Lead time for delivery of your canoe will depend on your location and the availability of the product. Our standard delivery time is 2-3 weeks. If you require your canoe sooner, please contact our customer service team to see if we can accommodate your request.
